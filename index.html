Location:

Joe's Garage, 443 Main St, Catskill, NY 12414

Getting there:

Amtrak runs regular trains from Penn Station to Hudson. From the station, it is a short walk into the town of Hudson, or a 10 minute taxi ride across the Hudson River to Catskill. Albany International Airport is the closest airport option to the area, but flies to and from limited cities. The venue is approximately a 2.5 hour drive from major NYC airports.

Lodging:

There are many hotels in the area surrounding Catskill, including Rivertown Lodge, The Barlow and The Wick in Hudson (directly across the Hudson River from Catskill) and The Stewart House in Athens. Additionally, there are a plethora of Airbnb and VRBO rentals in Catskill, and surrounding towns such as Athens, Leeds and Hudson.

We recommend that our guests book lodging in advance, as the Hudson Valley is a popular destination on spring weekends!
